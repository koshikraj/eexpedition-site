About Company

It All Began With One Incident

We started our journey when we noticed how schools needed an end-to-end provider of excursions for their students. With a simple vision of taking kids to destinations that they desired to visit, we worked for 3 years being a tour operator. Things took a turn when we noticed how the perspective of kids changed on something as small as 10 rupees, and something as large as water conservation through experiences during journeys. While we worked with schools where these excursions were optional, it wasn’t too hard to notice the stark difference between the students who traveled and students who didn’t.

What We Do Today

1

We made a simple pivot since then to introduce learning as they visited the destinations. This involved learning in detail about the destination they visited, the activities happening in the place, and its cultural evolution. The destinations comprised of 1-day experiences to 7-days experiences, both of which involved triggering the 4Cs of the young minds (Critical Thinking, Creativity, Collaboration and Communication).

2

We slowly figured that this is the key solution to one of India’s most pressing concerns - the education gap. While technology and societal constructs were advancing faster than ever, the education system in India hadn’t caught up with the pace.

3

And today, we are the one of the first initiators of this experience based learning concept wherein we provide curated off-campus learning experiences for school students. We handle the entire conceptualisation, planning, execution and management of all these programs so that schools need not dedicate in-house manpower and resources towards their off-campus excursions.

We are your one-stop education-based travel partner, with customised programs as per your needs.
